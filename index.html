Meet Marijana

The Heart Behind Every Design

Hi, I'm Marijana (Maja) Bandjak — the creator and designer behind Events by MJ. Based in Edmonton, Alberta, I specialize in custom balloon décor that transforms ordinary spaces into extraordinary celebrations.

Every piece I create is designed with intention — not just balloons, but statement décor that reflects the person being celebrated. From intimate birthday gatherings to grand corporate events, I pour my heart into every detail to make your occasion truly one of a kind.

Whether it's a milestone birthday, a baby shower, or a corporate grand opening, I work closely with each client to bring their vision to life through stunning colour palettes, personalized touches, and sculptural balloon artistry.

How to Book

Four Simple Steps to Your Dream Event

1

Reach Out

Send me a message via Instagram DM, email, or the contact form below with your event details and vision.

2

Design Together

We'll discuss your colour palette, theme, venue, and design preferences to create the perfect plan for your celebration.

3

Secure Your Date

A non-refundable retainer and signed service agreement secure your booking. Payment accepted via e-Transfer or cash.

4

Enjoy the Magic

I'll handle the setup, styling, and every beautiful detail — you just show up and celebrate!

Good to Know

  • Non-refundable retainer required to secure your date
  • Refundable damage deposit for rented equipment
  • Cancellations require 48+ hours advance notice
  • Balance due on or before setup day
  • Payment via e-Transfer or cash
  • Outdoor events may require backup indoor location
  • All balloons are yours to keep after the event
  • Arches, stands & frames are collected after takedown
