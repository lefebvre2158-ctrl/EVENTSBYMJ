Important Information

Balloon Care & Handling

Please follow these guidelines to keep your balloons looking their best

🖐 Handling Your Balloons

  • Always lift and carry your balloon arrangement from the base
  • Foil balloons are delicate — handle with care
  • Avoid squeezing or compressing the balloons
  • Do not re-tie, separate, or adjust any part of the arrangement

🏠 Placement & Environment

  • Place in a low-traffic area to minimise contact
  • Avoid staircases, hallways, and high-movement zones
  • Limit movement once placed — relocating risks damage
  • Keep away from heat sources and direct sunlight
  • Indoor use is strongly recommended

⚠️ Important Note

  • Once balloons are picked up or installation is complete, the client assumes full responsibility
  • Events by MJ cannot guarantee replacements or repairs after handoff
  • Following these care tips will help your balloons look beautiful throughout your event
💡 Tip: Balloons look their best within 8–12 hours of setup. Plan your timeline accordingly for the most stunning display!
